About Test Calgary

Dedicated to your success since our founding

Founded in 2015, Test Calgary emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a premier testing center that combines cutting-edge technology with genuine human care. Our founders recognized a significant gap in the market for testing facilities that prioritized both accuracy and the test-taker's experience. What began as a small operation with a single testing room has evolved into Calgary's most trusted assessment center, serving thousands of candidates annually. We've invested heavily in our infrastructure, training, and customer service to ensure that every individual who walks through our doors receives world-class treatment and achieves their testing goals with confidence and support.

Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve their full potential through comprehensive, accessible, and high-quality testing services. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, security, and professionalism in everything we do. We believe that testing should be a positive experience that catalyzes personal and professional growth. Our core values—excellence, integrity, compassion, and innovation—guide every decision we make. We continuously invest in the latest testing technologies, provide ongoing training for our staff, and actively seek feedback from our clients to improve our services. Test Calgary isn't just a testing center; we're a partner in your success, dedicated to helping you unlock your potential and achieve the goals that matter most to you.
